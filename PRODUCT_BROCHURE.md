## 2. Key Feature Showcase — What Makes Us Different

### 2.1  AI-Powered Recruitment — Find the Right People, Faster

**Smart CV Screening**
- Upload up to **25 resumes at once** (PDF, Word, or Text)
- The AI reads every resume like an experienced HR director
- Scores each candidate on **5 dimensions**: skills match, experience depth, education background, culture fit, and growth potential
- You decide what matters most — **adjust scoring weights** with simple sliders (e.g., "I care more about culture fit than education")
- Get a **ranked list** of candidates with detailed reports including:
  - Overall suitability score
  - Per-dimension scores with visual bars
  - Key strengths and weaknesses
  - Hiring risks to watch for
  - Suggested **interview questions** tailored to each candidate
  - Culture alignment analysis

**Interview Assistant**
- Paste any interview transcript into the system
- AI evaluates the candidate across **7 dimensions**: technical skills, communication, logical thinking, adaptability, teamwork, culture fit, learning ability
- Get a comprehensive report with:
  - Scores for each dimension
  - Candidate strengths and weaknesses
  - Red flags to investigate
  - Key quotes from the interview
  - Follow-up questions to ask
  - Clear hiring recommendation (Strongly Recommend / Recommend / Hold / Not Recommend)

**Company Culture Integration**
- Upload your company handbook, values, and policies
- The AI "learns" your company culture
- Every CV screening and interview evaluation considers **culture fit** — not just skills

### 2.2  AI Employee Insights — Know Your People

**AI Employee Chat (RAG-Powered)**
- Type a question about any employee in natural language
- Examples: "What are John's key strengths?", "How has Mary's attendance been this month?", "Who might be a flight risk?"
- The AI pulls data from **all available sources**: employee profile, salary history, attendance records, leave history, KPI performance, and uploaded CVs/profiles
- Get instant, data-driven answers — no more digging through files and spreadsheets
- Built-in quick actions:
  - **Risk Check** — spot potential retention risks
  - **Skills Review** — see what skills each employee has
  - **Attendance Check** — view attendance patterns

**Skill Extraction & Development Planning**
- The AI automatically identifies skills from employee CVs and profiles
- Recognizes over **40 skills** across technical, management, language, and soft skills categories
- Detects proficiency levels (Beginner / Intermediate / Advanced)
- Identifies skill gaps compared to target roles
- Recommends training courses to close those gaps
- Helps you build a data-driven development plan for every employee

**Employee Directory**

- Smart search and filtering by name, department, or status

**Payroll Processing**
- One-click monthly salary calculation

- Beautiful payslip generation — ready to send to employees
- Department-level payroll summary for budget planning
- **AI Salary Adjustment Suggestions** — based on KPI performance and years of service

## 5. Real-World Use Cases — How Companies Use Our Platform

### Use Case 1: Rapid Hiring for Expansion
> "We need to hire 5 engineers this quarter. We received 200 applications."

- HR uploads all 200 resumes to the CV Screening module
- Enters the job description and sets weights (emphasizing technical skills and experience)
- **In 5 minutes**, the AI ranks all 200 candidates with detailed analysis
- HR interviews the top 20, using the AI's suggested interview questions
- For each interview, the Interview Assistant evaluates the transcript
- **Result**: 5 hires in 3 weeks instead of 3 months

### Use Case 2: Annual Performance Review
> "We need to evaluate 50 employees for year-end bonuses and salary adjustments."

- HR opens the Employee Chat and asks about each employee: "Summarize John's performance this year"
- The AI pulls KPI scores, attendance records, and development progress
- Payroll module generates salary adjustment suggestions based on KPI + tenure
- **Result**: 50 reviews completed in 2 days instead of 2 weeks

### Use Case 3: Compliance & Leave Management
> "We need to ensure our leave policies comply with Taiwan Labour Standards Act."

- Annual leave is automatically calculated based on each employee's hire date
- Leave balances are tracked in real-time with visual progress bars
- Approval workflow ensures managers review and approve/reject requests
- **Result**: Zero compliance issues, employees self-serve leave requests

### Use Case 4: Employee Development
> "We want to identify skill gaps and create development plans for our team."

- HR uploads employee CVs and profiles
- The AI extracts skills from each document
- Gap analysis shows which skills are missing for target roles
- Course recommendations suggest training to close gaps
- **Result**: Data-driven development plans for every employee
